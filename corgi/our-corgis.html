Our Corgis

Luna

Luna is the mother of our corgi puppies. She is a young mother who is very sweet and loves to play fetch and cuddle. Her fur color is red. She is a purebred Pembroke Welsh Corgi.

Chester

Chester is the father of our corgi puppies. He is also a young father who loves to dress up and play with Luna. His fur color is sable. He is a purebred Pembroke Welsh Corgi as well.
